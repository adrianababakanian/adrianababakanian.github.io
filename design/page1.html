Cal Hacks 4.0

I'm a director at Cal Hacks, the largest hackathon in the world! One of my main roles is as graphic designer — logos, branding, marketing, you name it. Over the past year, I created the branding for Cal Hacks 4.0 under the general guidance of a California road trip theme.

Cal Hacks 4.0 was an event marketed to tens of thousands of people, and we ultimately ended up hosting 2000 people at the California Memorial Stadium. Badges, shirts, wristbands, banners, posters, maps, flyers, Snapchat filters, countdowns, decals, and more were all part of the agenda — below is a compilation of some of the finals products!
