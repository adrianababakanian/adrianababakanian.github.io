CubCon

One of our goals at Cal Hacks is to make hacker culture accessible to anyone who wants to be a part of it, regardless of level of expertise in programming and tech. In Spring 2017, we hosted our first ever CubCon, a series of talks, workshops, and breakout sessions hosting several industry speakers and prominent campus tech clubs geared torwards hackers with little or no experience. I chose to market the event in a pixelated, video-game-inspired style.
