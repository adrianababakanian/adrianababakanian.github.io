Cal Hacks Branding

I'm a director at Cal Hacks, the largest hackathon in the world! One of my main roles is as graphic designer — logos, branding, marketing, you name it. Over the past year, I created the branding for Cal Hacks 4.0 under the general guidance of a California road trip theme to celebrate the fact that advanced and beginner hackers alike come to California from all over the world to hack with us in Berkeley.

Every iteration of Cal Hacks gets dressed in a new bear named dd0ski that represents UC Berkeley's mascot Oski. I designed the Cal Hacks 4.0 dd0ski — this little guy was featured on thousands of shirts, swag bags, badges, wristbands, and more!
